# Mail Tool (Transactional Emails)

**Menu Path:** `Tools > Mail`

The **Mail Tool** is the engine responsible for dispatching automated, transactional emails to your users (e.g., Order Confirmations, Password Resets).

> **Note:** The technical SMTP credentials (how the emails actually leave your server) are configured in `Workspace Settings > Communication Settings`. This module focuses on the *content* and *logs* of those emails.

## Reviewing Email Logs

It is inevitable that a customer will call support saying, "I never got my order confirmation." This module is where you investigate.

1. Navigate to the Mail Tool's **Logs** tab.
2. Search by the customer's email address.
3. **Delivery Status:** Check the status column.
   - *Sent/Delivered:* The email successfully reached their inbox. (Tell them to check their spam folder).
   - *Bounced:* Their inbox is full, or they made a typo in their email address during checkout (e.g., `@gamil.com`).
   - *Failed:* There is a configuration issue with your SMTP server.

## Sending Manual Emails
While mostly automated, administrators can use this tool to compose and send a manual email to a specific user directly from the CMS, using your official corporate template.
