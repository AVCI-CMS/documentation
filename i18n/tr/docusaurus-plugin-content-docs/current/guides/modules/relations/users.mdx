# Users & Customers (CRM)

**Menu Path:** `Relations > Users`

The **Users** module is your Customer Relationship Management (CRM) hub. It lists every individual who has registered an account on your platform, whether they are a standard shopper, a merchant, or an administrator.

## Managing the User Directory

When you open the Users list, you can search for customers by Name, Email, or Phone Number.

### Viewing a Customer Profile
Clicking on a user's row opens a 360-degree view of their interactions with your platform:
- **Order History:** See a list of every purchase they've made, their lifetime value (LTV), and average order size.
- **Saved Addresses:** View or delete their saved shipping and billing addresses.
- **Wallet Balance:** Check if they have any remaining store credit.
- **Support Tickets:** See a history of their communications with your support team.

## Administrative Actions

As an admin, you can perform several actions on a user's account:
- **Password Reset:** If a user complains they cannot log in, click the **"Send Password Reset Email"** button to dispatch a secure reset link to their registered email.
- **Role Assignment:** Elevate a standard shopper to a "Merchant" or "Admin" by changing their assigned Role in the settings tab.
- **Account Ban:** If a user is engaging in fraudulent behavior, change their status to **"Banned"** or **"Suspended"**. They will immediately lose access to their account, and their active sessions will be terminated.
