# Organizations (B2B Accounts)

**Menu Path:** `Relations > Organizations`

The **Organizations** module is designed for B2B (Business-to-Business) e-commerce architectures. It allows you to group multiple individual [Users](./users.mdx) under a single corporate umbrella.

## Understanding Corporate Accounts

In a B2B scenario, a single company might have multiple employees placing orders on your site. Organizations allow you to manage them logically.

- **Shared Billing:** All users assigned to "Company XYZ" can place orders that are automatically billed to Company XYZ's corporate tax ID and billing address.
- **Tiered Pricing:** You can assign a specific discount tier (e.g., "Wholesale - Tier 1") to an Organization. Every user linked to this organization will automatically see the discounted prices instead of the retail prices.
- **Credit Limits:** Instead of paying by credit card, an Organization might have a monthly "Net 30" credit limit. Employees draw from this shared credit pool when checking out.

## Creating an Organization

1. Click **"Create"** in the Organizations list.
2. **Corporate Details:** Enter the Company Name, Tax ID, and official corporate address.
3. **Assign Members:** Search for existing Users in your system and link them to this organization as "Employees" or "Purchasing Managers".
4. **Set Financial Rules:** Define their credit limit or assign them to a specific B2B pricing group.

Once saved, the assigned users will experience a customized, corporate checkout flow the next time they log in.
