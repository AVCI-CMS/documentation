# Edit Brand

**Menu Path:** `Relations > Brands > Edit`

To modify a brand, click the **"Edit"** button next to its name in the Brands list.

## Making Updates

- **Logo Replacement:** If a brand undergoes a visual rebrand, you can upload the new logo here. The change will instantly cascade across all product pages and "Shop by Brand" carousels on the frontend.
- **Description Updates:** Expand the brand's history or update SEO keywords in the description field.

## Deactivating a Brand

If you stop carrying a brand entirely, you might want to hide it from your site navigation without deleting the historical data.
1. Change the status toggle to **Inactive**.
2. Click **"Save"**.
3. **Note:** Deactivating a brand does *not* delete or deactivate the products associated with it. However, the brand name will no longer appear as a clickable filter option in the sidebar of category pages.
