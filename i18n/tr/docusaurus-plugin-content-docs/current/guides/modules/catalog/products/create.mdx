# Create Product

**Menu Path:** `Catalog > Products > Create`

Adding a new product involves providing all the essential details that your customers need to make a purchase decision. The creation screen is divided into logical sections.

## Step 1: Basic Information
This section forms the foundation of your product page.

- **Product Name:** Enter a clear, descriptive title. This is the main `<h1>` tag on the frontend and is crucial for SEO.
- **Category:** Select the appropriate category from the hierarchical tree. Proper categorization ensures customers can find the product via site navigation.
- **Brand:** If applicable, link the product to an existing brand defined in the `Relations > Brands` module.
- **Short Description:** A concise summary (usually 1-2 sentences) that appears near the top of the product page or in product cards.
- **Detailed Content (Description):** Use the Rich Text Editor to provide comprehensive details, specifications, usage instructions, and embedded media.

## Step 2: Inventory & Identification
Proper identification is vital for warehouse management and order fulfillment.

- **SKU (Stock Keeping Unit):** A unique alphanumeric code used internally to track inventory. **This field is mandatory and must be unique.**
- **Barcode (EAN/UPC):** The standard retail barcode number. Useful for point-of-sale integrations and shipping logistics.
- **Initial Stock Quantity:** Enter the number of items currently available for sale. If you track inventory dynamically, the system will deduct from this number upon successful orders.

## Step 3: Media Gallery
High-quality visuals directly impact conversion rates.

- **Cover Image:** The primary image shown in catalogs and search results.
- **Gallery Images:** Upload additional photos showing different angles, close-ups, or lifestyle shots. You can drag and drop to reorder the images.
- **Video Link:** (Optional) Provide a YouTube or Vimeo link to embed a product showcase video.

## Step 4: Finalizing and Saving
Once the core information is entered:
1. Set the initial **Status** (Draft or Active).
2. Click the **"Create"** or **"Save"** button at the bottom of the page.

> **Next Steps:** Saving the basic product unlocks advanced configuration tabs. To set prices, add product variations (like size or color), and configure SEO, proceed to the [Edit Product](./edit.mdx) guide.
