# Products List

**Menu Path:** `Catalog > Products`

The **Products** module is the core of your catalog management. This screen provides a centralized view of every physical, digital, or service-based product available in your store or marketplace.

## Navigating the Product Data Table

When you access `Catalog > Products`, you are presented with a highly functional Data Table. This interface is designed to help you quickly locate, filter, and manage large inventories.

### 1. Advanced Filtering and Search
The top bar of the data table includes powerful search and filtering capabilities:
- **Global Search:** Type a keyword (Product Name, SKU, or Barcode) into the search bar to instantly filter the list.
- **Column Filters:** Click on specific column headers (like *Category* or *Brand*) to narrow down the results.
- **Status Filter:** Easily toggle between `Active` (currently visible to customers), `Draft` (work in progress), and `Inactive` (hidden or out of stock) products.

### 2. Quick Actions & Status Management
You do not always need to open the full edit screen to make changes:
- **Status Toggle:** Use the quick switch next to a product to instantly activate or deactivate it.
- **Bulk Actions:** Select multiple products using the checkboxes on the left. Once selected, a bulk action menu appears allowing you to delete, archive, or change the status of many items at once.

### 3. Row Actions
At the far right of each product row, you will find an actions menu (represented by three dots `...` or an edit icon). Click here to:
- **Edit:** Open the comprehensive product editing screen.
- **Delete:** Permanently remove the product from the catalog.
- **Duplicate:** Clone the product to save time when creating similar items.

## Creating a New Product
To expand your catalog, click the prominent **"Create"** button located at the top right of the screen. For a step-by-step guide on filling out the creation form, please refer to the [Create Product](./create.mdx) documentation.
