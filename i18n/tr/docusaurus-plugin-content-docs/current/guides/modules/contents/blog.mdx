# Blog Management

**Menu Path:** `Contents > Blog`

The **Blog** module is the section where you can publish and manage articles, guides, and long-format texts on your platform. Its SEO-friendly structure makes it easier for your content to stand out in search engines.

## How to Add a New Blog Post?

1. Navigate to the Blog management screen by following the **Contents > Blog** path from the left menu.
2. Click the **"Create"** button in the top right corner.
3. Fill in the following basic information on the screen that appears:
   - **Title:** The main heading of your post.
   - **Content:** Format your text, add images or videos using the Rich Text Editor.
   - **Featured Image:** Upload the main cover photo that will appear in the blog listing.
   - **Category / Tags:** Assign your post to relevant categories and determine keywords (tags).
4. Do not forget to fill in the **Meta Title** and **Meta Description** of your post from the SEO settings tab (if any).
5. When your operations are finished, publish your post by clicking the **"Save"** or **"Publish"** button.

## How to Edit or Delete a Blog Post?

- **Editing:** On the blog list screen, you can update the content by clicking the **"Edit" (Pen icon)** button to the right of the post you want to change.
- **Changing Status:** Instead of completely deleting posts, you can unpublish them by changing their status to "Draft" or "Inactive". You can use the "Status" switch on the edit screen to do this.

> **Important Tip:** Regularly checking your SEO metadata (URL slug, Title) greatly contributes to your content ranking better on Google and other search engines.
