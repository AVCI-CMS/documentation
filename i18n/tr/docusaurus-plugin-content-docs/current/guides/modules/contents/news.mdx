# News

**Menu Path:** `Contents > News`

The **News** module is the area where you can quickly publish latest developments, press releases, and short internal/external announcements related to your brand.

Its main difference from blog posts is that news are generally time-focused, shorter contents that convey instant developments and are listed in a separate "Press / Announcements" section on the site.

## How to Publish a New News Item?

1. Click on the **Contents > News** link from the left menu.
2. Press the **"Create"** button on the incoming list page.
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **News Title:** The striking headline of your announcement.
   - **Excerpt:** A 1-2 sentence summary that will appear below the title on listing pages.
   - **Content:** The main body of the news.
   - **Publish Date:** The official date the news was published (If you want to give a past or future date, you can set it here).
4. After adding the relevant media file (Cover photo), click the **"Publish"** button.

## Managing News

Via the news list;
- You can see the reading statistics of published news (if configured),
- You can change the status of expired or obsolete news to **"Inactive"**.
- You can enter news in multiple languages (if i18n is supported) by translating them separately for each language.
