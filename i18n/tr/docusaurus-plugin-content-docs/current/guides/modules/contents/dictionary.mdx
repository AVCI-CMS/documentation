# Dictionary

**Menu Path:** `Contents > Dictionary`

The **Dictionary** is the area where you centrally manage the translations and definitions of the static texts (e.g., "Add to Cart", "Login", "Read More" button texts) on the frontend of your application.

Thanks to this module, you can easily change button names or warning messages on your site for all languages without needing to know coding or requiring developer support.

## How to Add and Translate a Dictionary Key?

1. Click on the **Contents > Dictionary** link from the left menu.
2. Click the **"Add New"** button on the page.
3. Fill in the following fields in the translation form:
   - **Key:** The code-side equivalent of the text (e.g., `button.add_to_cart`). It should generally be in a standard format given to you by your developer or predefined.
   - **Values:** Write the equivalents (e.g., TR: "Sepete Ekle", EN: "Add to Cart") of the relevant key for the active languages on your site (e.g., Turkish, English, German).
4. Add the dictionary item to the system by pressing the **"Save"** button.

## Searching and Updating the Dictionary

Over time, hundreds of words can accumulate in your dictionary.
- Using the search bar, you can search for the text you want to change or the "Key" value.
- You can update the existing translation by clicking the **Edit** icon next to the word you want to edit. Changes will generally reflect instantly on the frontend of your site (if there is no caching).
