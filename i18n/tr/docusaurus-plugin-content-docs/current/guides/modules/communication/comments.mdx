# Comments & Reviews

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Comments`

The **Comments** module is a central moderation hub. It collects all user-generated text submitted across your platform—whether it is a 5-star product review, a comment on a blog post, or feedback on an event.

## Why Moderate Comments?
User-generated content drives SEO and builds trust, but it can also attract spam or inappropriate language. Moderating ensures that only high-quality, relevant content appears on your public pages.

## The Moderation Workflow

When you open the Comments list, you will see a unified feed of all incoming reviews.

### 1. Status Toggles
Comments generally enter the system in a **"Pending"** state (unless your Workspace settings allow auto-approval).
- Read the content of the comment.
- If it is constructive, click **"Approve"**. It will immediately go live on the relevant product/page.
- If it contains spam or abusive language, click **"Reject"** or **"Delete"**.

### 2. Contextual Information
Next to each comment, you will see exactly where it was posted:
- **Linked Entity:** A clickable link showing the specific Product (e.g., "Red T-Shirt") or Blog Post.
- **Author:** The name of the registered User or the guest email address.
- **Rating:** If the comment is a product review, you will see the star rating attached to it.

### 3. Administrator Replies
Often, a negative review can be turned into a positive experience if a merchant or admin replies publicly.
- Click **"Reply"** on a comment.
- Type your response (e.g., "We apologize for the sizing issue, please contact support for a free exchange.").
- Your reply will be threaded below the customer's comment on the frontend, visually distinguished as an "Official Response".
