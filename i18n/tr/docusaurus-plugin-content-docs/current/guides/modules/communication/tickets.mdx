# Tickets (Helpdesk)

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Tickets`

The **Tickets** module is an integrated helpdesk system. It allows logged-in customers (and your merchants) to open structured support requests regarding their orders, payments, or technical issues.

## Why Use Tickets Instead of Email?
Tickets keep communication organized. A ticket is tied directly to a User's profile and, optionally, to a specific Order ID. This gives your support agents immediate context without having to ask the customer for their order number.

## The Ticket Workflow

### 1. Triage and Assignment
When a customer opens a ticket on the frontend, it appears in this list with an **"Open"** status.
- **Subject & Category:** E.g., "Where is my order?", Category: "Logistics".
- **Priority:** High, Medium, or Low.
- **Assignee:** A manager can assign the ticket to a specific support agent on your team so they take ownership of the issue.

### 2. Conversational Interface
Clicking into a ticket opens a chat-like thread.
- **Read the Issue:** See the customer's initial message and any attached photos (e.g., a picture of a damaged product).
- **Reply:** Type your response in the editor. When you hit send, the customer is notified via email and can reply directly from their "My Account" dashboard on the frontend.
- **Internal Notes:** You can write private notes (highlighted in yellow) that only other admins can see (e.g., "Waiting on warehouse manager to confirm stock").

### 3. Resolution
Once the issue is solved:
- Change the ticket status to **"Closed"** or **"Resolved"**.
- This removes it from the active queue, keeping your support team focused on open issues. If the customer replies again, the ticket will automatically reopen.
