# Contact Forms Inbox

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Contact Forms`

The **Contact Forms** module acts as a simplified inbox for all messages submitted through the standard "Contact Us" page on your website's frontend.

## Managing the Inbox

Instead of having website inquiries sent blindly to a corporate email address where they might get lost in spam, AVCI CMS stores them securely in the database.

### 1. Reading Messages
- The list view shows the sender's name, email, subject line, and the date submitted.
- Click a row to read the full body of the message.

### 2. Taking Action
- **Reply:** You can reply directly to the customer's email address from your mail client by clicking their email link.
- **Mark as Read / Resolved:** Once an inquiry has been handled (e.g., a sales question was answered), change its status to **"Resolved"** or **"Archived"** to keep your inbox clean and focus on pending issues.

> **Spam Prevention:** If you are receiving a high volume of automated spam through the Contact Form, consider enabling Google reCAPTCHA in the `Workspace Settings > Advanced Settings` module.
