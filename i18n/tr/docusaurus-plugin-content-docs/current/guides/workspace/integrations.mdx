# App Integrations

**Menu Path:** `Integrations`

While Webhooks and APIs require developer effort, the **Integrations** module is a 1-click App Store that connects AVCI CMS to popular third-party software.

## Connecting Services

Browse the available categories to find the integration you need. Common examples include:

### Marketing & Analytics
- **Google Analytics 4 / Tag Manager:** Paste your Measurement ID to start tracking visitor behavior, page views, and e-commerce conversions automatically.
- **Meta (Facebook) Pixel:** Connect your pixel to track ad ROI and build retargeting audiences.

### Accounting & ERP
- **Logo / Netsis / Xero:** Connect your accounting software. When an invoice is generated in AVCI CMS, it is automatically synced to your accounting ledger, eliminating manual data entry.

### Customer Support
- **Zendesk / Intercom:** Add a live chat widget to your storefront by simply pasting the App ID.

*To install an app, click on it, enter the required credentials (usually an API key or an OAuth login flow), and toggle it to "Active".*
