# Workspace Overview

**Menu Path:** `Workspace Settings > Overview`

The **Overview** section acts as the administrative dashboard specifically for the system-level health and billing of your active Workspace. It is typically only accessible to the "Owner" of the workspace.

## Monitoring Usage and Quotas

Most CMS and cloud platforms bill based on usage. This screen provides full transparency into your consumption.

### 1. Subscription Plan
- **Current Plan:** See if you are on the "Starter", "Pro", or "Enterprise" tier.
- **Upgrade/Downgrade:** If you are hitting your limits, you can initiate a plan upgrade directly from this screen.

### 2. Live Usage Metrics
Visual progress bars show you how close you are to your limits for the current billing cycle:
- **Storage Used:** E.g., "45GB of 50GB used". If you are near the limit, you may need to visit the [Media Library](../tools/media.mdx) tool to delete old files.
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** How many unique users logged into your platform this month.
- **API Calls:** If you have external apps integrated, track how many requests they are making.

## Billing and Invoices
At the bottom of the overview, you will find a ledger of your relationship with AVCI CMS (not your customers' invoices, but *your* invoices).
- You can download past PDF receipts for your accounting department.
- You can update the primary credit card on file used to pay for your AVCI CMS subscription.
