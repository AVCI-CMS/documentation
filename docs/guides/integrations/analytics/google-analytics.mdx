# Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Track traffic and e-commerce conversions on your storefront.

## Setup
1. In your Google Analytics account, create a Web Data Stream.
2. Copy the **Measurement ID** (starts with `G-`).
3. In AVCI CMS, go to **Integrations > Analytics**.
4. Paste the Measurement ID.
5. The CMS automatically injects the GA4 snippet into your storefront and fires standard e-commerce events like `view_item`, `add_to_cart`, and `purchase`.
