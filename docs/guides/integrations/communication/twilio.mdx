# Twilio Integration

Twilio allows you to send SMS messages globally.

## Setup
1. From your Twilio Console, copy your **Account SID** and **Auth Token**.
2. Identify your Twilio Phone Number or Alphanumeric Sender ID.
3. In AVCI CMS, navigate to **Workspace > Settings > Communication > SMS**.
4. Select **Twilio**.
5. Enter your Account SID, Auth Token, and Sender ID.
6. **Note:** If sending SMS to international numbers, ensure your Twilio Geo-Permissions allow routing to those countries.
