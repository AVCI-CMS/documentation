# Amazon SES Integration

Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) is a highly scalable and cost-effective bulk and transactional email-sending service.

## Setup
1. Log in to your AWS Console and open the SES dashboard.
2. Verify your domain identity and DKIM settings.
3. Generate SMTP Credentials (Username and Password) via IAM.
4. In AVCI CMS, navigate to **Workspace > Settings > Communication > Email**.
5. Select **Custom SMTP**.
6. Enter the AWS SMTP endpoint (e.g., `email-smtp.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com`), port (usually 587), and your generated credentials.
