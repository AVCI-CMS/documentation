# 1. Applicant Info

This step captures the personal details of the human being who is registering the merchant account (the legal representative).

- **Identity Verification:** The `Identity Number` (e.g., TC Kimlik No) is required and validated algorithmically.
- **Contact Details:** Valid mobile phone numbers and email addresses are mandatory for OTP verification and legal notices.
- **Residential Address:** Must be the applicant's official registered address.
