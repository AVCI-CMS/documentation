# 2. Business Info

This step captures the corporate entity details. The form behaves dynamically based on the **Corporation Type** selected:

### Personal / Private Companies (Şahıs Şirketleri)
- The **Tax Number** (Vergi No) is automatically synchronized with the Applicant's Identity Number (TC Kimlik).

### Limited / Joint Stock Companies (Limited / A.Ş.)
- Requires a distinct 10-digit **Tax Number**.
- Requires a valid **Mersis Number**.
- Requires a valid **KEP Address** (Registered Electronic Mail) for legal government correspondence.
