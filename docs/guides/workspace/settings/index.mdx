# Workspace Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings`

The **Settings** module is the central command center for your entire workspace. It is divided into logical sub-categories that allow you to configure everything from basic visual themes to complex financial and relational rules. 

Navigating through these settings is essential for tailoring the AVCI CMS to exactly fit your business model.
