# SMS Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Communication > SMS`

:::info Permission Requirements
To configure SMS settings, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:Manage** permission.
:::

The SMS Settings page allows you to configure your SMS provider and map message templates for critical alerts, marketing, or Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) codes.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. Service Provider
- **Select Provider:** Choose your active SMS gateway provider (e.g., Verimor). 
- **Prerequisite:** Just like email, the SMS provider must first be integrated and authenticated via the [Integrations](../../../integrations/index.mdx) module before it can be selected here.

### 2. Message Templates
To standardize outgoing messages, you link system events to specific template IDs provided by your SMS gateway:
- **Merchant Templates:** Automated texts sent to sellers (e.g., new order alerts, payout notifications).
- **User Templates:** Automated texts sent to end-users (e.g., phone verification codes, delivery updates).

> [!TIP]
> **Cost Control:** SMS messaging carries a per-message cost. Only assign templates to critical alerts (like OTPs or shipping dispatches) to manage your operational expenses effectively.

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** Phone verification codes are not being delivered.
- **Solution:** First, verify that the SMS Service Provider is selected. Second, ensure that the exact Template ID for the OTP/Verification message is correctly inputted under the "User Templates" section. Finally, check the Global Notification settings to ensure the SMS channel is active.
