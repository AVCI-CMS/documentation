# Dispute Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Sale Settings > Dispute`

:::info Permission and Plan Requirements
To use this feature, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:View** permission and the **MARKETPLACE** plan.
:::

Dispute Settings allow you to manage the conflict resolution processes between customers and merchants. You can configure when a dispute can be opened, what evidence is required, and the auto-resolution timelines.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. General Dispute Policy
- **Dispute System (allowDispute):** Enables or disables the global dispute functionality. If disabled, customers must handle issues through standard customer service channels rather than opening a formal platform dispute.
- **Auto-Resolve Duration (autoResolveDays):** Specifies the number of days the system will wait before automatically resolving a dispute in favor of the customer if the merchant fails to respond.
- **Evidence Requirement (requiredEvidence):** Mandates customers to upload photo or video evidence when opening a dispute, preventing baseless claims.
- **Appeals (allowAppeals):** Allows the losing party (merchant or customer) to appeal the final dispute decision to the platform administrators.

### 2. Allowed Dispute Reasons
You can toggle which specific reasons customers can select when initiating a dispute:
- **PRODUCT_DEFECT:** The item arrived damaged or broken.
- **NOT_AS_DESCRIBED:** The item does not match the description or photos.
- **DELIVERY_DELAY:** The shipping took significantly longer than promised.
- **FAKE_PRODUCT:** The item is suspected to be a counterfeit or replica.

> [!WARNING]
> Disabling a specific dispute reason will prevent customers from selecting it moving forward, but it will not affect any ongoing active disputes that were opened under that reason.

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** The dispute automatically resolved in favor of the customer too quickly.
- **Solution:** Increase the **Auto-Resolve Duration** (e.g., to 7 or 14 days) to give your merchants adequate time to review and respond to the claim.
