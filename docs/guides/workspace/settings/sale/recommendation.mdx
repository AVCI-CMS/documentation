# Recommendation Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Sale Settings > Recommendation`

:::info Permission and Plan Requirements
To use this feature, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:View** permission and the **ECOMMERCE, MARKETPLACE** plan.
:::

Recommendation Settings manage the logic and display of product suggestions across your platform, helping to drive upselling and cross-selling through automated algorithms.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. Recommendation Modules
Enable or disable specific types of product suggestions:
- **Cross-Sell (allowCrossSell):** Suggests complementary items (e.g., suggesting a phone case when viewing a phone).
- **Up-Sell (allowUpSell):** Suggests premium or higher-tier alternatives to the currently viewed item.
- **Related Products (allowRelatedProducts):** Displays items from the same category or brand.
- **Recently Viewed (allowRecentlyViewed):** Shows the user a personalized history of items they've interacted with.

### 2. Display Limits & Algorithms
- **Maximum Recommendations (maxRecommendations):** Limits the number of items displayed in a single recommendation carousel or block (e.g., max 10 items) to prevent UI clutter.
- **Algorithm Preference (algorithm):** Determines the underlying logic used to fetch related items, such as Category-based matching or Tag-based semantic matching.

> [!TIP]
> Effectively utilizing the **Cross-Sell** and **Up-Sell** modules has a direct impact on increasing the Average Order Value (AOV). Ensure your product catalog has accurate tags and categories to fuel the recommendation algorithms properly.

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** The Related Products section is empty for certain items.
- **Solution:** The item might not share a category or any tags with other active products in the catalog. Check the selected **Algorithm Preference** and verify the product's metadata.
