# Merchant Relation Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Relations > Merchant`

:::info Permission Requirements
To configure Merchant Relation settings, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:Manage** permission.
:::

The Merchant Relation module is the core configuration area for defining how merchants (sellers) operate within your marketplace. It encompasses onboarding rules, commission defaults, and operational limitations.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. Onboarding & Registration
- **Auto-Approval:** Determine if new merchant registrations are automatically approved or if they require manual KYC (Know Your Customer) review.
- **Default Commission Rate:** Set the baseline percentage commission that the workspace takes from this merchant's sales. This can be overridden on a per-merchant basis later.

### 2. Component Settings
Control the visibility and requirement status of fields on the Merchant Profile form.
- **Tax & Legal Information:** Make Tax ID and Company Registration fields strictly mandatory to comply with local regulations.
- **Contact Info:** Enforce phone number and support email requirements for customer service purposes.

### 3. Operational Restrictions
- **Payout Threshold:** Set the minimum balance a merchant must accumulate before a payout is triggered.
- **Staff Limits:** Restrict the number of sub-users/staff a single merchant can invite to their own dashboard.

> [!WARNING]
> Changing the "Default Commission Rate" only applies to newly registered merchants. Existing merchants will retain their historical commission rates unless manually updated.

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** New merchants are bypassing the document upload step during registration.
- **Solution:** In the Component Settings table on this page, ensure that the "Legal Documents" field is toggled as "Required".
