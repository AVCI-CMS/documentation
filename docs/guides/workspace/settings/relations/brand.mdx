# Brand Relation Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Relations > Brand`

:::info Permission Requirements
To configure Brand Relation settings, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:Manage** permission.
:::

The Brand Relation settings allow you to define the operational constraints, default behaviors, and limitations for brand entities interacting within your workspace ecosystem.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. General Constraints
- **Maximum Active Brands:** Define the upper limit of brands that can be simultaneously active under a single merchant or organization.
- **Verification Requirement:** Toggle whether newly registered brands require manual admin verification before their products can be listed.

### 2. Form & Component Settings
You can customize which fields are visible or mandatory when an administrator or merchant is creating a new brand profile.
- **Logo & Cover Image:** Enforce the uploading of high-resolution branding assets.
- **Brand Story / Description:** Make the descriptive text field mandatory to ensure all brands have a complete profile.

### 3. Restriction Settings
- **Product Listing Limits:** Restrict the maximum number of distinct products a brand can list across the marketplace.
- **Category Exclusivity:** Optionally limit a brand to publish products only within specific predefined categories.

> [!TIP]
> Ensure that any changes made to form field requirements (like making a logo mandatory) are communicated to your merchants, as this might block them from creating new brands if they lack the required assets.

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** A merchant is unable to create a new brand.
- **Solution:** Check the "Maximum Active Brands" restriction. If the merchant has reached the limit defined here, they will be blocked from creating additional brands unless the limit is increased.
