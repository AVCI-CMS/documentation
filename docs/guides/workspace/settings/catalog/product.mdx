# Product Settings

**Menu Path:** `Workspace > Settings > Catalog Settings > Product`

:::info Permission and Plan Requirements
To use this feature, you must have the **Workspace:Settings:View** permission and the **ECOMMERCE, MARKETPLACE** plan.
:::

Product Settings manage the workspace-wide configuration for product sales, approval mechanisms, component visibility, data restrictions, and financial commissions associated with physical, digital, or service products.

## Key Features & Configuration

### 1. General Product Settings
Manage product sales activity, approval processes, and base limits.
- **Global Sales Status (isProductSalesEnabled):** Toggles the ability to sell products on the platform.
- **Publish Approval (requireApproval):** When enabled, newly created or updated products remain in a "Draft" status until an administrator approves them.
- **Max Products Per Merchant:** Restricts the total number of products a single merchant can list.
- **Product Variants (allowProductVariants):** Enables size, color, or other variant selections for products.
- **Image Support (allowProductImages & maxImagesPerProduct):** Controls whether merchants can upload images and limits the number of images per product.
- **Allowed Product Types (productTypes):** Select which types of products are permitted on your platform (e.g., PHYSICAL, DIGITAL, SERVICE).

### 2. Component Management
Customize the visibility and mandatory status of fields within the product creation form.
- You can toggle whether fields like `Gallery`, `Featured Image`, `Description`, `Stock`, `Price`, and `Category` are shown to users and whether they are required.

### 3. Data Restrictions
Set limits on the data that can be entered for products.
- **Price Limits:** Define the `Minimum Price` and `Maximum Price`.
- **Stock Limits:** Define the `Minimum Stock` and `Maximum Stock`.
- **Text Lengths:** Control the maximum character limits for the Title, Description, and Specifications to ensure consistent UI formatting across your storefront.

### 4. Financial Configuration (Commission)
Set the default and limit commission rates for product sales.
- **Default Rate:** The global commission percentage applied to product sales.
- **Min/Max Rates:** The lower and upper bounds for commission rates.
- **Custom Allowance:** Activating this allows assigning specific, custom commission rates on a per-merchant basis.

### 5. Role-Based Visibility
Fine-tune which specific fields and components are visible to different team members or merchants based on their assigned roles.

> [!WARNING]
> Changes made to the product settings do not retroactively affect existing products; they only apply to newly created or updated products. 

## Common Issues

- **Issue:** Merchants cannot upload product images.
- **Solution:** Verify that **Image Support** is enabled and the **Max Images** limit is greater than 0 in the General Settings tab.
