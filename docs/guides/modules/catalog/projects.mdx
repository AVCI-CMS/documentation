# Projects (Portfolio)

**Menu Path:** `Catalog > Projects`

The **Projects** module functions as a portfolio management system. It is designed for B2B service providers, agencies, architects, and freelancers who need to showcase their past work, case studies, or ongoing developments.

Unlike products, projects are generally not "purchased" directly but are used to build trust and demonstrate expertise to potential clients.

## Detailed Project Creation

To add a new showcase piece, click **"Create"** in the Projects list.

### 1. Project Metadata
- **Project Title:** The name of the campaign, building, or software developed.
- **Client/Employer:** Who commissioned the work.
- **Completion Date:** Helps build a chronological timeline of your firm's growth.
- **Category:** Tag the project (e.g., "Web Design", "Interior Architecture", "Consulting").

### 2. The Case Study Content
A successful project page tells a story.
- **The Challenge:** Describe the client's initial problem.
- **The Solution:** Explain the services provided and the methodology used.
- **The Results:** Highlight measurable outcomes, ROI, or testimonials. Use the Rich Text Editor to structure this narrative.

### 3. Media Gallery
Visuals are paramount in a portfolio.
- Upload a strong **Cover Image**.
- Add a **Gallery** of high-quality before/after photos, screenshots, or architectural renders.

## Managing the Portfolio
From the Projects data table, you can easily drag and drop items to define a specific display order (Featured Projects) on your frontend, ensuring your best work is seen first.
