# Catalog

The **Catalog** module is the commercial heart of your platform. This is where you build your inventory, manage what you are offering to your users, and control pricing and availability.

Whether you are selling physical goods, digital downloads, ticketing events, or showcasing real estate projects, the Catalog provides the specialized tools to manage them all centrally.

### Modules in the Catalog Group

- **Products:** Manage physical, digital, and service-based items. Control pricing, stock, variants, and shipping.
- **Events:** Create physical or online events. Manage ticketing, sessions (recurring or serial), venues, and capacity limits.
- **Projects:** Showcase large-scale portfolios such as real estate developments, software projects, or architectural models.
- **Campaigns:** Define global discount rules, buy-one-get-one offers, and basket-level promotions.
- **Coupons:** Generate specific discount codes to be shared with users for targeted marketing.
- **Routes:** Create map-based itineraries, travel routes, or geographic guides.
