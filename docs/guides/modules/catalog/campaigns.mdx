# Campaigns & Promotions

**Menu Path:** `Catalog > Campaigns`

The **Campaigns** module is a powerful promotional engine that allows you to automate complex discount rules across your entire catalog or specific segments, without needing to manually edit individual product prices.

## Advanced Campaign Mechanics

When you click **"Create"**, you enter the campaign builder, which operates on a "Condition -> Action" logic.

### Step 1: Definition & Timing
- **Campaign Name:** An internal and external identifier (e.g., "Summer Clearance 2026").
- **Schedule:** Set a precise Start Date/Time and End Date/Time. The system will automatically enable and disable the discounts exactly at these moments. No manual intervention required.

### Step 2: Setting Conditions (The "If")
Who or what does this campaign apply to?
- **Target Selection:** Apply to the "Entire Store", "Specific Categories" (e.g., Only Shoes), "Specific Brands", or "Specific Products".
- **Cart Thresholds:** (Optional) Apply the campaign only if the user's cart total exceeds $100.
- **User Groups:** (Optional) Restrict the campaign to specific user roles (e.g., "Wholesale Customers Only" or "VIP Members").

### Step 3: Setting Actions (The "Then")
What happens when the conditions are met?
- **Percentage Discount:** Reduce the price of eligible items by X% (e.g., 20% off all Shoes).
- **Fixed Amount Discount:** Deduct a flat $X amount.
- **Buy X Get Y (BOGO):** Complex logic where adding 3 items charges the price of 2.

> **Priority Management:** If a product falls under multiple active campaigns, the system relies on the **Priority Score** you assign. The campaign with the highest priority will override others to prevent accidental double-discounting.
