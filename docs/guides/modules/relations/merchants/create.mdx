# Create Merchant

**Menu Path:** `Relations > Merchants > Create`

If you are not relying on an automated frontend application process, or if you are onboarding a VIP partner, you can manually create a merchant account from the backend.

## Step-by-Step Manual Onboarding

Click **"Create"** on the Merchants list page to open the registration form.

### 1. Corporate Identity
You must establish the legal entity of the business.
- **Legal Company Name:** As it appears on their tax documents.
- **Tax Office & Tax Number:** Crucial for generating automated commission invoices.
- **Corporate Address:** The physical location of the business headquarters.

### 2. Store Details
This is what the end-customers will interact with.
- **Store Name:** E.g., "Avci Electronics".
- **Store Slug:** The URL identifier (e.g., `/store/avci-electronics`). It must be unique.
- **Store Description:** A brief bio for the merchant's profile page.
- **Logos & Banners:** Upload the visual branding for the merchant's storefront.

### 3. Financial & Commission Settings
Define how money flows between the platform and this merchant.
- **Commission Rate (%):** Set a specific percentage you will take from their sales. Leave blank if you use global category-based commissions.
- **Bank Details (IBAN):** Enter the bank account where the merchant will receive their Payouts from the [Wallet](../../finance/wallet.mdx).

### 4. User Assignment
A "Merchant" is just a business entity. A human needs to log in to manage it.
- Enter the **Email Address** of the store owner. The system will automatically create a [User](../users.mdx) account for them and link it to this Merchant entity. They will receive an email to set their password.
