# Merchants List

**Menu Path:** `Relations > Merchants`

The **Merchants** module is the backbone of any marketplace setup. It serves as the directory and management interface for all independent sellers, vendors, or B2B partners operating under your platform's umbrella.

## Understanding the Merchant Directory

When you open `Relations > Merchants`, you see a comprehensive data table of all businesses registered to sell on your platform.

### Key Data Points
- **Store Name:** The public-facing name of the merchant.
- **Corporate ID / Tax Number:** Essential for billing and legal compliance.
- **Status:** Shows whether a merchant is currently allowed to sell (`Active`), waiting for approval (`Pending`), or banned (`Suspended`).
- **Commission Rate:** A quick view of the percentage the platform takes from this specific merchant's sales.

## Merchant Onboarding Flow

In most marketplaces, merchants apply to join via a frontend registration form. When they submit their application, they appear in this list with a **"Pending"** status.

1. **Review Application:** Click the edit/view icon next to a Pending merchant.
2. **Verify Documents:** Check the tax certificates or IDs they uploaded during registration.
3. **Approve:** If everything is in order, change their status to **Active**. They will automatically receive an email notifying them that their store is open, and they can begin listing products.

## Impersonation (Login As)
Administrators often need to troubleshoot issues a merchant is experiencing on their specific dashboard.
- Click the **"Login As"** button in the row actions to temporarily assume the merchant's identity. This allows you to see exactly what they see without needing their password.
