# Relations

The **Relations** module group acts as the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) core of your platform. This is where you track, manage, and engage with every entity that interacts with your business—be it a standard end-user, a corporate merchant selling on your marketplace, or the physical locations where your events take place.

By centralizing these profiles, you can easily tie a customer's history to their orders, payments, and support tickets.

### Modules in this Group

- **Users:** Manage end-customers. View their profiles, address books, purchase histories, and manage their account status (Active/Suspended).
- **Merchants:** Control the vendor profiles for marketplace models. Approve new merchants, set commission rates, and manage their store settings.
- **Organizations:** Group multiple users under corporate accounts. Useful for B2B models where an organization pays for its employees.
- **Places:** Manage physical venues, stores, or event locations complete with map coordinates, capacities, and address data.
- **Brands:** Maintain a global list of product brands. This ensures data consistency (e.g., preventing variations like "Nike" and "NIKE" from fragmenting the catalog).
- **Contact Forms:** Review and reply to inbound inquiries submitted by visitors via your website's contact pages.
