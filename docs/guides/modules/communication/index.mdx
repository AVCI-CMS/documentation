# Communication

The **Communication** module group is dedicated to how your platform speaks to its users, and how users speak back to you.

Whether you are broadcasting a marketing campaign via email, sending an urgent push notification about a delivery, moderating product reviews, or handling customer support complaints, all interactions are centralized here.

### Modules in this Group

- **Newsletter (Mailing):** Design, schedule, and send mass email marketing campaigns to your subscribers.
- **Notifications:** Send targeted alerts (In-App, Push, or SMS) to specific user groups or individuals.
- **Comments & Reviews:** Moderate user-generated content. Approve, reject, or reply to product reviews and blog comments.
- **Tickets (Helpdesk):** A complete customer support ticketing system to track, assign, and resolve user issues and inquiries.
