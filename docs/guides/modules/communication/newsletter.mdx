# Newsletter (Mailing)

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Newsletter`

The **Newsletter** module allows you to design, schedule, and send mass email communications. It is an essential tool for re-engaging users, announcing new products, or distributing coupon codes.

## Creating an Email Campaign

1. Navigate to **Communication > Newsletter**.
2. Click **"Add New"** to draft a new campaign.

### 1. Campaign Details
- **Subject Line:** The headline that appears in the user's inbox.
- **Target Audience:** Select who should receive this email. You can target all subscribers, specific user groups, or even users who haven't logged in for a certain period.
- **Schedule:** Choose to send the email immediately or schedule it for a future date and time.

### 2. Email Content (Design)
- Use the rich text editor to compose your message. 
- You can embed images, promotional banners, and call-to-action buttons (e.g., "Shop the Sale").
- *Best Practice:* Always include an unsubscribe link at the bottom of your templates to comply with anti-spam regulations (GDPR/KVKK).

## Managing Subscribers
- The module also maintains a list of all active email subscribers. 
- If a user clicks "Unsubscribe" from their email, the system automatically flags their profile and prevents future marketing emails from being sent to them.
