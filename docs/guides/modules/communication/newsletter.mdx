# Newsletter Subscribers

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Newsletter`

The **Newsletter** module manages the database of users and guests who have explicitly opted-in to receive marketing communications from your brand.

## Growing the Subscriber Base
Users typically enter this list via:
- The footer "Subscribe" input on your website.
- A checkbox during the checkout process ("Keep me updated on news and offers").
- Lead generation popups.

## Managing the List

### 1. Monitoring Opt-ins
The data table displays the email addresses, the date they subscribed, and their current status.
- **Active:** Currently receiving emails.
- **Unsubscribed:** Users who clicked the "Unsubscribe" link in the footer of a marketing email. *Do not manually switch these back to active, as it violates anti-spam laws.*

### 2. Exporting for Campaigns
While AVCI CMS can send transactional emails, you will likely use a dedicated marketing platform (like Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or SendGrid) for sending visual newsletters to thousands of people.
- Click the **"Export CSV"** button to download your active subscriber list.
- Import this CSV into your external email marketing tool to launch your campaign.
