# Comments

**Menu Path:** `Communication > Comments`

The **Comments** module allows you to view, moderate, and reply to user-generated content across your platform. This includes product reviews, event feedback, and blog post comments.

## Moderation Workflow

To ensure high-quality content and filter out spam or inappropriate language, comments often require moderation before they are visible to the public.

1. Go to **Communication > Comments**.
2. New comments typically appear with a status of *PENDING*.
3. Click on a comment to review it.

### Review Actions
- **Approve:** The comment becomes visible on the frontend (e.g., on the Product's detail page).
- **Reject:** The comment is hidden and permanently flagged. Use this for spam or terms-of-service violations.
- **Delete:** Completely removes the record from the database.

## Replying to Comments

Engaging with your users builds trust. If a user leaves a negative review about a product, you can write an official reply directly from this module.
- Your reply will appear nested under the user's original comment on the frontend, usually highlighted as an "Official Response".

*Note for Marketplaces:* If a comment is specifically about a Merchant's product, the respective Merchant can also log into their dashboard and reply to the comment. The Platform Admin retains the power to moderate both the user's comment and the Merchant's reply.
