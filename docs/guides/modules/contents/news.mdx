# News

**Menu Path:** `Contents > News`

The **News** module is structurally identical to the Blog module but serves a fundamentally different purpose on your frontend. It is intended for corporate announcements, official press releases, event highlights, and timely company updates.

Separating News from the Blog allows you to:
1. Maintain completely different visual layouts on the frontend (e.g., a formal, list-based press room vs. a highly visual, grid-based blog).
2. Segment your SEO strategies and XML sitemaps.
3. Manage different author permissions (e.g., PR team manages News, Content team manages Blog).

## Creating a News Announcement

The workflow for publishing a news piece is the same as writing a blog post.

1. Navigate to **Contents > News**.
2. Click **"Add New"**.
3. Fill out the properties:

### 1. Basic Info
- **Title:** The headline of the press release.
- **Status:** You can prepare press releases in advance as *Drafts* and set them to *Published* right when the news embargo lifts.
- **Category:** Categorize news items (e.g., "Press Releases", "Company Updates", "In the Media").
- **Slug:** The URL structure.
- **Abstract:** A brief executive summary of the announcement.

### 2. Media
- **Featured Image:** The primary image (like a company logo or an event photo) representing the news.
- **Gallery:** Upload additional media, such as event coverage photos or product launch shots.

### 3. Content (Rich Text)
- The body of your press release. Use the editor to format your text, insert quotes from executives, and provide detailed corporate updates.

### 4. SEO
- Optimize the news piece so journalists and stakeholders can easily find your announcements on Google.

4. Click **"Save"** to instantly publish your news to the dedicated Press/News section of your platform.
