# Questions (Q&A)

The Q&A module allows you to manage questions users ask about products, merchants, or events. While all questions are usually listed under **Communication** in the main menu, only the questions belonging to a specific **Product** or **Merchant** are listed in their respective detail views.

## Question List and Filtering

For each question in the list, you can find the following information:
- **User:** The name and avatar of the person asking the question. A blue dot appears next to new (unanswered) questions.
- **Content:** The text of the question.
- **Category:** The type of question (General, Shipping, Payment, Product Info, Warranty, Return, Technical, Pricing, Availability, Other, etc.)
- **Status:** Pending, Answered, Closed, Hidden, Deleted.
- **Answered:** Indicates whether the question has received a response (Answered / Unanswered).

## Answering and Managing Questions

### 1. Question Details and Answering
When you click the "View" button or the row from the list, the detail panel of the question opens.
- The top of the panel contains information about the user who asked the question, the question's category, and its current status.
- If the question hasn't been answered yet, a text area appears at the bottom. You can type your response here and inform the user by clicking the **Send Answer** button.
- Answers provided by an administrator or an authorized merchant automatically display an **Official** badge next to them.

### 2. Bulk Actions
By selecting multiple questions from the list (using checkboxes), you can perform the following bulk actions:
- **Delete:** Removes the selected questions from the system.
- **Moderate:** Updates the approval status of the questions and publishes them.
- **Update Status:** Changes the status of selected questions in bulk (e.g., to "Closed", "Hidden").

> **Tip:** When you delete a question, an "Undo" button briefly appears in the bottom right corner. You can restore accidentally deleted questions within this short timeframe.
