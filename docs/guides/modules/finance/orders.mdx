# Order Management

**Menu Path:** `Finance & Logistics > Orders`

**Orders** is the nerve center of your e-commerce operations. This module tracks the lifecycle of every purchase made on your frontend, from the moment a customer checks out to the final delivery.

## The Order Lifecycle

When you access the Orders list, you see a data table sorted by the most recent purchases. The most crucial column here is the **Status**, which dictates what needs to happen next.

### Standard Order Status Flow:
1. **Pending Payment:** The order is placed, but the payment gateway hasn't confirmed receipt of funds yet (e.g., awaiting wire transfer).
2. **Processing / Preparing:** Payment is confirmed. The warehouse or merchant should now pack the items.
3. **Shipped:** The package has been handed over to the courier. A tracking number is usually generated here.
4. **Delivered:** The customer has received the package.
5. **Cancelled:** The order was aborted before shipping (by customer request or payment failure).
6. **Refunded / Returned:** The customer sent the item back after delivery.

## Processing an Order

Click the **"View"** or **"Manage"** button next to an order to open the Order Details screen.

### 1. Verifying Details
- **Customer & Address:** Double-check the shipping and billing addresses.
- **Items:** Review the exact variants (e.g., "Red-XL T-Shirt") and quantities purchased.
- **Fraud Check:** If your payment provider flags the transaction as high-risk, it will be highlighted here.

### 2. Updating Status & Notifying the Customer
To move an order forward:
1. Click the **Update Status** dropdown in the top right.
2. Select the next logical step (e.g., change from "Preparing" to "Shipped").
3. **Tracking Information:** If changing to "Shipped", a modal will prompt you to select the Courier Company and input the Tracking Number.
4. Once saved, the CMS automatically triggers an email/SMS to the customer containing their tracking link.

### 3. Handling Modifications
Sometimes customers make mistakes. Administrators have the power to edit orders *before* they ship.
- **Edit Address:** Fix typos in the shipping address.
- **Remove/Add Items:** If an item is unexpectedly out of stock, you can remove it from the order and issue a partial refund directly from this screen.

> **Note on Cancellations:** Changing an order status to "Cancelled" does not automatically refund the credit card in all setups. Always verify with the [Payments](./payments.mdx) module to ensure the funds were successfully reversed.
