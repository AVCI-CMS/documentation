# Penalties

**Menu Path:** `Finance & Logistics > Penalties`

The **Penalties** module is designed exclusively for Marketplace operators. It allows you to enforce platform rules by applying financial deductions to merchants who violate your terms of service.

## Why Apply a Penalty?

Common reasons for penalizing a seller include:
- **Late Shipping:** Failing to dispatch an order within the promised SLA (Service Level Agreement).
- **High Return Rates:** Sending defective or counterfeit products.
- **Chargeback Losses:** Passing the cost of a lost bank dispute onto the negligent seller.

## How to Issue a Penalty

1. Navigate to **Finance & Logistics > Penalties** and click **"Create"**.
2. **Select Merchant:** Choose the offending seller from the dropdown list.
3. **Amount:** Enter the exact monetary value of the fine.
4. **Reason (Internal & External):** 
   - *Internal Note:* For your admin team's eyes only.
   - *External Note:* The explanation the merchant will see on their dashboard (e.g., "Penalty for failing to ship Order #12345 within 48 hours").
5. **Save & Execute:** Once saved, the CMS automatically creates a deduction record in the [Transactions](./transactions.mdx) ledger and removes the funds from the merchant's [Wallet](./wallet.mdx) Available Balance.

> **Caution:** Use penalties judiciously. Over-penalizing sellers can lead to high churn rates on your marketplace. Always ensure your merchant agreements clearly state the conditions under which financial penalties can be applied.
