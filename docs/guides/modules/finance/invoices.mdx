# Invoices & Billing

**Menu Path:** `Finance & Logistics > Invoices`

The **Invoices** module allows you to view, generate, and manage all billing documents created by the platform. This is crucial for B2B operations and tax compliance.

## Types of Invoices

Depending on your business model, the system generates different invoices:
- **Customer Invoices:** Sent to the end-buyer when an order is completed.
- **Commission Invoices:** In a marketplace, the platform issues an invoice to the Merchant for the commission fees deducted from their sales.
- **Subscription Invoices:** If merchants pay a monthly fee to use your platform, those recurring invoices appear here.

## Managing Invoice Records

### Viewing and Downloading
When you click on an invoice record:
- You will see the total amounts, tax breakdown (VAT/GST), and line items.
- Click the **"Download PDF"** button to get a printable version of the invoice to send to a client manually.

### Generating Invoices Manually
If you do not have auto-generation enabled via an e-invoicing provider (like e-Fatura systems):
1. Go to the Order detail page.
2. Click **"Generate Invoice"**.
3. Verify the billing address and tax numbers.
4. Save, and the invoice will now appear in this module's list.

### Invoice Statuses
- **Draft:** Created but not yet finalized or sent to the tax authority.
- **Issued:** Finalized and legally binding.
- **Cancelled:** If an order is completely refunded, the associated invoice must often be cancelled (or a credit note must be issued, depending on local tax laws).
