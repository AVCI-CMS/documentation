# SMS

**Menu Path:** `Tools > SMS`

The **SMS** module is an essential utility for both transactional communications and marketing. Due to the high open-rates of text messages, SMS is the preferred method for urgent notifications (like OTP verification or delivery updates).

## Configuring the SMS Gateway

Before you can send any messages, you must link the CMS to your external SMS Provider (e.g., Twilio, Netgsm, Mutlucell).

1. Navigate to **Tools > SMS** and open the **Settings** tab.
2. Enter your **Provider Name**.
3. Input the **API Key** and **Secret Key** provided by your telecommunications company.
4. Set the **Sender ID (Header)**. This is the alphanumeric name (up to 11 characters, e.g., "AVCI_CMS") that appears on the user's phone instead of a standard phone number. Note: In many regions, this Sender ID must be officially registered and approved by the government.

## Sending a Mass SMS Campaign

Similar to the [Newsletter](../communication/newsletter) module, you can broadcast marketing texts:
1. Click **"New SMS Campaign"**.
2. **Select Audience:** Filter by specific User Groups, active customers, or paste a list of raw phone numbers.
3. **Message Body:** Type your message. Keep it concise. A standard SMS is 160 characters; exceeding this will cost you 2 or more SMS credits per user.
4. **Schedule:** Send immediately or pick a future date.

## Managing SMS Templates
For automated system alerts (like "Your order #123 is shipped"), you can create predefined templates.
- Go to the **Templates** tab.
- You can use dynamic variables in your templates, such as `{{USER_NAME}}` or `{{TRACKING_LINK}}`. The CMS will automatically swap these variables with real data before sending.
