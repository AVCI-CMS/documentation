# Invoices

**Menu Path:** `Finance & Logistics > Invoices`

The **Invoices** module manages the official billing documents (like E-Fatura/E-Arşiv) required for tax compliance and accounting. While the *Orders* module shows what a customer bought, the *Invoices* module proves the legal financial transaction.

## Generating or Uploading an Invoice

Invoices are strictly tied to Orders (or Sub-Orders in a Marketplace).

1. Go to **Finance & Logistics > Orders** and open an Order detail page.
2. Locate the **Invoice Manager** card.
3. Depending on your system's integration, you have two options:

### Option A: Manual Upload
If you generate invoices using external accounting software (e.g., Logo, Paraşüt, Xero):
1. Export the PDF invoice from your external software.
2. In the Order's Invoice Manager, click **Upload Invoice**.
3. Select the PDF file. Once uploaded, the customer can instantly download it from their "My Orders" page on the frontend.

### Option B: Automatic Generation (API Integration)
If your platform is integrated with an E-Fatura provider:
1. Click the **Generate E-Invoice** button.
2. The system automatically sends the customer's billing address, tax number, and purchased items to the government tax portal or integrator.
3. A verified PDF is returned and attached to the order automatically.

## Viewing All Invoices

To see a global list of all invoices issued across the platform:
1. Navigate to **Finance & Logistics > Invoices**.
2. Here you can filter invoices by Date, Merchant, or Amount.
3. This list is highly useful for end-of-month accounting reconciliations. You can bulk download these PDFs to send to your accountant.
