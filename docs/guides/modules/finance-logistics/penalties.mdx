# Penalties

**Menu Path:** `Finance & Logistics > Penalties`

The **Penalties** module is primarily used in Marketplace models where Platform Administrators need to enforce rules and maintain quality standards among Merchants.

## What is a Penalty?

A penalty is an administrative fine levied against a merchant for violating platform policies. Examples include:
- Shipping an order 3 days late.
- Selling counterfeit goods.
- High customer dispute rates.

When a penalty is applied, it creates a specific *PENALTY* transaction on the Merchant's ledger. This amount is automatically deducted from the merchant's *Withdrawable Balance* in their Wallet.

## Issuing a Penalty

1. Navigate to **Finance & Logistics > Penalties**.
2. Click **"Add New"** to issue a fine.
3. **Select Merchant:** Choose the offending merchant from the list.
4. **Amount:** Enter the exact monetary value of the fine.
5. **Reason:** Provide a clear, detailed explanation. This reason is visible to the merchant in their Wallet dashboard.
6. **Related Order (Optional):** If the fine is tied to a specific botched delivery, link the Order ID here for reference.
7. Click **"Save"**. The system immediately debits the merchant's wallet.
