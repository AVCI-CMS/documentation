# 3. Document Upload

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Organization > Step 3`

To activate an Organization account, robust legal verification is mandatory.

## Required Uploads

Organizations must upload official corporate documents, which typically include:
- **Certificate of Incorporation / Trade Registry Gazette:** Proof that the company legally exists.
- **Tax Registration Plate/Certificate:** Official document showing the company's tax status.
- **Signature Circular:** A notarized document showing who has the legal authority to sign on behalf of the company.

:::info Verification Delay
Because Organization accounts are granted high-level privileges (like creating unlimited sub-merchants), the manual review process for these documents by the platform's compliance team may take up to 3-5 business days.
:::
