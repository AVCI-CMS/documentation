# 2. Business Information

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Organization > Step 2`

This step requires the legal and operational details of the parent company.

## Corporate Details

- **Company Legal Name:** The exact name as registered in the national trade registry.
- **Tax/Registration ID:** The primary corporate tax identification number.
- **Headquarters Address:** Full legal address of the corporate headquarters.

## Billing Entity
- **Separate Billing Address:** Organizations often have a different address for invoicing purposes. If so, a distinct billing address must be provided here.

> [!WARNING]
> The Tax ID provided in this step will be used for all master invoices generated by the platform. Any errors here will require manual intervention by the platform's finance team to correct.
