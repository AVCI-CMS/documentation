# Organization Setup Overview

Welcome to the Organization Onboarding and Setup guide. 

Organizations are the highest-level entity in the platform, typically representing a holding company, a franchise parent, or an enterprise-level B2B client that manages multiple merchants or brands underneath it.

## Onboarding Steps

To legally operate as a parent organization on the platform, the following steps must be completed in the initial onboarding wizard:

1. **Applicant Information:** Identifying the primary executive or administrator.
2. **Business Info:** Collecting corporate registration and headquarters details.
3. **Documents:** Uploading enterprise-level legal documentation.
4. **Bank Details:** Setting up the primary corporate account for aggregated payouts.
5. **Declaration:** Signing the enterprise agreement.

> [!TIP]
> Since Organizations can have multiple sub-entities (merchants/brands) attached to them, ensuring the accuracy of this root-level information is critical for tax and invoicing purposes.
