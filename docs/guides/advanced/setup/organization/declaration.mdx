# 5. Final Declaration

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Organization > Step 5`

The final step is the acceptance of the enterprise-level agreements.

## Process

- **Enterprise SLA & Terms:** The applicant must review and accept the enterprise Service Level Agreement, data processing addendums, and general terms.
- **Signatory Confirmation:** The applicant confirms they are legally authorized (as per the Signature Circular uploaded in Step 3) to bind the company to these terms.

### Submission
Once submitted, the Organization's status becomes `PENDING_REVIEW`.

:::info Restricted Access
Until the Workspace Admin approves the organization, the dashboard will remain in a restricted "read-only" state. You will not be able to invite team members or create sub-merchants until approval is granted.
:::
