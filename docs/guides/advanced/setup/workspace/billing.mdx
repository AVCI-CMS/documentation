# 2. Billing & Activation

To activate your chosen Blueprint, you must enter a valid payment method.

- **Secure Payment:** Credit card data is securely tokenized via Stripe or iyzico.
- **Free Trials:** If your selected plan includes a trial period, your card will not be charged immediately, but it is required to prevent spam and abuse.
- **Cancel Anytime:** Subscriptions can be managed later from the `Workspace > Billing` settings.
