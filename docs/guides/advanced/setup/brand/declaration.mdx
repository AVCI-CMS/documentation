# 5. Final Declaration

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Brand > Step 5`

The Declaration is the final step of the brand onboarding wizard. It serves as the digital legal handshake between the brand entity and the platform.

## Process

- **Terms of Service:** The applicant must review and accept the platform's overarching terms and the specific Brand Agreement.
- **Accuracy Pledge:** The applicant digitally signs a statement guaranteeing that all uploaded documents, bank details, and business information are accurate and legally binding.

### Submission
Once the declaration is checked, the applicant can click the **Submit Application** button. 

:::info What happens next?
If "Auto-Approve Brands" is disabled in the workspace settings, the brand's status will change to `PENDING_REVIEW`. The applicant will be locked out of the catalog management features until a Workspace Admin approves the submitted documents.
:::
