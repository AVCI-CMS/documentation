# Brand Setup Overview

Welcome to the Brand Onboarding and Setup guide. 

This section covers the necessary steps a brand manager must complete when first registering on the platform. The onboarding flow requires capturing legal, financial, and descriptive data to ensure the brand complies with platform regulations and marketplace standards.

## Onboarding Steps

The brand onboarding wizard consists of five primary steps:

1. **Applicant Information:** Identifying the primary contact person.
2. **Business Info:** Collecting brand descriptors and legal status.
3. **Documents:** Uploading necessary brand registration/trademark documents.
4. **Bank Details:** Setting up the payout destination.
5. **Declaration:** Final legal consent and submission.

> [!TIP]
> **Approval Process:** Depending on the `Brand Relation` settings configured by the Workspace Admin, newly registered brands might require manual verification before their catalog becomes visible on the storefront.
