# 3. Document Upload

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Brand > Step 3`

To prevent intellectual property infringement, brands must verify their identity and legal right to operate under the specified trademark.

## Required Uploads

Depending on local regulations and the workspace configuration, brands typically need to upload:
- **Trademark Registration Certificate:** Proof of brand ownership.
- **Authorization Letter:** If the applicant is a distributor rather than the direct owner, a signed letter of authorization from the brand owner is required.
- **Tax Clearance/Company Registration:** Basic corporate identity proofs.

:::info File Formats
The platform accepts `.pdf`, `.jpg`, and `.png` files. Ensure all documents are clear and readable to avoid delays in the manual verification process.
:::

## Common Issues
- **Issue:** Document upload fails repeatedly.
- **Solution:** Check the file size. Most workspaces restrict individual document uploads to a maximum of 5MB or 10MB to optimize storage.
