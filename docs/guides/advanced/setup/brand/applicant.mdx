# 1. Applicant Information

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Brand > Step 1`

The first step in the brand onboarding process captures the details of the individual applying on behalf of the brand.

## Required Information

- **Full Name:** The legal name of the applicant.
- **Role/Title:** The applicant's position within the brand (e.g., Brand Manager, CEO, Authorized Signatory).
- **Contact Details:** Email address and a verified phone number for emergency contact regarding the application.

:::info Verification
The contact email provided here will be used for all critical onboarding communications, including document rejection notices or final approval confirmations.
:::

## Common Issues
- **Issue:** The applicant cannot proceed to the next step.
- **Solution:** Ensure all mandatory fields are filled and that the phone number matches the required international formatting standard.
