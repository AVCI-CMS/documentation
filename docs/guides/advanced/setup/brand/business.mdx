# 2. Business Information

**Route:** `(setup) > Setup > Brand > Step 2`

The Business Information step captures the corporate and descriptive details of the brand itself. This information is vital for the storefront presentation.

## Required Details

- **Brand Name:** The public-facing name of the brand as it will appear to customers.
- **Brand Story / Description:** A rich-text description detailing the history and values of the brand. (Note: Whether this is mandatory depends on the Workspace's *Brand Relation* settings).
- **Categories:** The primary market segments the brand operates in (e.g., Apparel, Electronics, Beauty).

> [!WARNING]
> **Category Lock:** Selecting the correct categories during onboarding is crucial. If the workspace enforces category exclusivity, the brand will only be able to publish products within the categories selected here.

## Brand Assets
- **Logo:** The brand's primary logo (SVG or high-res PNG recommended).
- **Cover Image:** A wide banner image used for the brand's dedicated storefront page.
