# Brand Portal Overview

**Route:** `(brand) > /brand`

Welcome to the Brand Portal. This dedicated environment allows brand owners and managers to control how their brand is represented across the platform, manage their verified product catalogs, and oversee authorized sellers.

## Getting Started

If your brand has not yet completed the initial registration wizard, please refer to the [Brand Setup Guide](../setup/brand/index.mdx).

## Core Modules

The brand portal is built around the following essential modules:

- **[Profile](./profile):** Control the brand's public story, logo, and banner imagery.
- **[Documents](./documents):** Manage trademark registrations and legal authorizations.
- **[Settings](./settings):** Configure brand exclusivity, catalog restrictions, and notifications.
- **[Taxes & Legal](./taxes):** View the corporate tax identity linked to the brand.
- **[Team Management](./team):** Invite brand managers and catalog specialists to collaborate.

> [!TIP]
> **Catalog Authority:** Depending on the platform's configuration, only verified brand managers may have the authority to create new product definitions (Master Products) for the brand. Merchants will then bind their stock to your verified products.
