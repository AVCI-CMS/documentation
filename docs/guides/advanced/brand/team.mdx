# Team Management

**Route:** `(brand) > /brand/team`

Invite your colleagues and staff to manage the brand's presence on the platform.

## Delegating Access

To invite a new team member, click **Invite Member** and enter their corporate email address.

## Available Roles

- **Brand Admin:** Full access to all settings, documents, and team management features.
- **Catalog Manager:** Can create, edit, and archive product definitions, but cannot alter legal documents or settings.
- **Marketing Specialist:** Can only edit the Brand Profile (Story, Logo, Banner) to run promotional campaigns.

:::info Isolation
Users invited here will strictly only have access to this specific Brand Portal. They cannot view the workspace dashboard or any merchant panels.
:::
