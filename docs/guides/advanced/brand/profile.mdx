# Brand Profile

**Route:** `(brand) > /brand/profile`

Your brand profile determines how your brand is perceived by shoppers. A complete profile increases consumer trust and visibility.

## Profile Assets

- **Brand Story:** Use the rich-text editor to tell the history of your brand, your mission, and your core values. This is displayed on your dedicated brand page.
- **Brand Logo:** Upload a high-resolution SVG or PNG logo with a transparent background.
- **Cover Image:** A high-quality lifestyle or product banner image (16:9 ratio).

## Public Contact Information
- Provide a public support email or phone number if you wish to offer direct customer support alongside your authorized retailers.

:::info Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
The Brand Story you write here is often indexed by search engines. Ensure it includes relevant keywords associated with your brand's primary products.
:::
