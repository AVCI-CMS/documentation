# Taxes & Legal Identity

**Route:** `(brand) > /brand/taxes`

This page contains the immutable corporate and financial identity of the entity that registered the brand.

## Managed Information

- **Corporate Identity:** The registered Company Name and Tax ID (VKN/TCKN) tied to this brand account.
- **Payout Bank Account:** If the platform pays out brand royalties or direct brand sales, the designated IBAN is managed here.

> [!WARNING]
> Changes to the Tax ID or Legal Company Name usually require manual intervention by the Workspace Admin, as this represents a legal transfer of the brand account to a different corporate entity.
