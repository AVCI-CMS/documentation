# Merchant Profile

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant/profile`

The Profile section allows merchants to manage how their store appears to customers on the storefront.

## Key Features

- **Store Name:** The public-facing name of your store.
- **Store Description:** A rich-text area to describe your business history, return policies, or specialties.
- **Visual Assets:** Upload or update your store's Logo (1:1 ratio recommended) and Cover Banner (16:9 ratio recommended).
- **Contact Info:** Publicly visible customer support email and phone number.

:::info Cache Invalidation
Changes made to your profile assets (like the logo) may take a few minutes to reflect on the live storefront due to Content Delivery Network (CDN) caching.
:::
