# Store Settings

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant/settings`

The Settings page houses the operational configuration for your specific store.

## Configuration Areas

- **Operating Hours:** Set the days and hours your store is open. Customers attempting to place local-delivery or pickup orders outside these hours will be blocked.
- **Vacation Mode:** Temporarily hide your entire catalog or disable checkouts if you are away on holiday or unable to fulfill orders.
- **Notifications:** Opt-in or out of SMS or Email alerts for new orders, low stock warnings, or payout confirmations.
- **Developer API (If Enabled):** Generate personal access tokens to connect your merchant account to external ERPs or accounting software.

:::info Feature Availability
Some settings, such as API access, might be hidden depending on the pricing tier or permissions granted to your merchant account by the Workspace Admin.
:::
