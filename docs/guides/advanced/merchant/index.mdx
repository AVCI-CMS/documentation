# Merchant Portal Overview

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant`

Welcome to the Merchant Portal. This isolated dashboard is designed specifically for individual sellers, store owners, and retail branches to manage their daily e-commerce operations. 

Unlike the Workspace panel (which manages the entire platform), the Merchant Portal is restricted to the data and settings of a single merchant entity.

## Getting Started

If you are a new merchant and have not yet completed your store registration, please refer to the [Merchant Setup Guide](../setup/merchant/index.mdx).

## Core Modules

The merchant settings route group includes the following core management modules:

- **[Profile](./profile):** Update public store details, cover photos, and contact information.
- **[Documents](./documents):** Manage KYC files, tax certificates, and compliance uploads.
- **[Settings](./settings):** Configure operational hours, notification preferences, and API integrations.
- **[Taxes & Finance](./taxes):** View billing addresses, payout bank accounts, and e-invoicing settings.
- **[Team Management](./team):** Invite staff members (e.g., cashiers, inventory managers) to your specific store.

> [!TIP]
> **Data Isolation:** All actions performed within this portal are strictly isolated. A merchant cannot see the orders, products, or settings of any other merchant on the platform.
