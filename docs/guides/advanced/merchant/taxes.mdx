# Taxes & Financial Settings

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant/taxes`

Manage the financial backbone of your store, including payout routing and tax compliance.

## Key Sections

### 1. Billing & Tax Identity
- View and update your registered Tax ID (VKN/TCKN) and Tax Office.
- Update your legal billing address for invoices generated by the platform.

### 2. Payout Account
- Manage the IBAN / Bank Account where your sales revenue (minus commissions) will be deposited.
- Note: Changing this bank account usually triggers a security hold until the new account is verified.

### 3. E-Invoicing (e-Fatura)
- Connect your e-invoice integrator credentials (if supported by the platform) so invoices can be automatically issued to customers upon order completion.

> [!WARNING]
> Mismatches between your Tax Identity and your Bank Account Holder Name will cause your payouts to fail and bounce back from the banking network.
