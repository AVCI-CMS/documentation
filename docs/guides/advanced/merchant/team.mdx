# Team Management

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant/team`

The Team module allows a merchant owner to delegate tasks by inviting staff members to the merchant portal.

## Inviting Staff

You can invite new staff members via their email address. Upon accepting the invitation, they will have access *only* to your merchant portal, not the broader workspace.

## Roles and Permissions

When inviting a team member, you must assign them a role:
- **Store Manager:** Full access to all merchant settings, including financial data.
- **Inventory Clerk:** Can add or edit products and update stock levels, but cannot view financial payouts.
- **Fulfillment Agent:** Can only view and update order statuses (e.g., preparing, shipped).

:::info Seat Limits
Your store may have a maximum limit on the number of staff members you can invite. If you reach this limit, you must contact the Workspace Admin to upgrade your capacity.
:::
