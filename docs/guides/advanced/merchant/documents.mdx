# Document Management

**Route:** `(merchant) > /merchant/documents`

The Documents module is a secure vault for your legal and KYC (Know Your Customer) files.

## Managing Documents

- **Document Status:** Track the verification status of your uploaded files (`PENDING`, `APPROVED`, or `REJECTED`).
- **Update Expired Files:** If a document like a Tax Certificate expires, the platform will prompt you to upload a renewed version here.
- **Additional Requests:** The Workspace Admin may request additional ad-hoc documents (like an identity proof for a new company director), which will appear here as a pending requirement.

> [!WARNING]
> If a critical document expires and is not replaced within the grace period, your store may be automatically suspended, preventing new sales.
