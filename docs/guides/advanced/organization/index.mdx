# Organization Portal Overview

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization`

Welcome to the Organization Portal. This high-level dashboard is designed for Enterprise and B2B clients who act as an umbrella "Parent" entity over multiple sub-merchants or brands.

## Purpose

The Organization Portal allows regional managers, franchise owners, or holding companies to manage cross-entity reporting, consolidated billing, and enterprise staff without logging into individual merchant panels.

## Core Modules

- **[Profile](./profile):** Update corporate details and headquarters information.
- **[Documents](./documents):** Manage enterprise-wide legal documents (e.g., Signature Circulars).
- **[Settings](./settings):** Configure cross-tenant access rules and API rate limits.
- **[Taxes & Billing](./taxes):** Manage the master billing address and consolidated payout accounts.
- **[Team Management](./team):** Invite regional directors who can oversee multiple attached merchants.

> [!TIP]
> **Sub-Entity Creation:** Depending on the platform's configuration, Organization Admins may have the ability to create new merchants directly from this portal, bypassing the standard public onboarding process.
