# Corporate Profile

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization/profile`

The Organization Profile section captures the high-level details of the enterprise.

## Corporate Details

- **Enterprise Name:** The holding or corporate name.
- **Headquarters Address:** The primary physical address of the parent company.
- **Corporate Contact:** A centralized support or contact email for the enterprise (often used by the platform's account managers).

:::info Visibility
Unlike Merchant or Brand profiles, the Organization profile is generally not visible to end-consumers on the storefront. It is used strictly for B2B relationship management and internal reporting.
:::
