# Consolidated Taxes & Billing

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization/taxes`

Manage the financial identity of the parent company.

## Master Billing

- **Tax ID & Office:** The corporate Tax ID used for master invoicing.
- **Master Payout Account:** If your platform agreement involves aggregated payouts (i.e., all sub-merchant earnings are paid directly to the holding company's bank account), you manage that primary IBAN here.

:::info Invoice Routing
The platform will generate SaaS/Commission invoices addressed to the information provided on this page, even if the sales were generated by individual sub-merchants.
:::
