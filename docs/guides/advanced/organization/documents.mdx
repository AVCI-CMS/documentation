# Corporate Documents

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization/documents`

As an enterprise entity, maintaining up-to-date legal documentation is critical to prevent service interruptions across all attached sub-merchants.

## Required Documents

- **Trade Registry Gazette / Certificate of Incorporation:** Proof of the holding company's legal existence.
- **Signature Circulars:** Notarized documents proving who has the authority to sign binding agreements on behalf of the entire organization.

> [!WARNING]
> If an organization's primary legal documents expire and are marked as invalid by the compliance team, *all* attached sub-merchants may have their payouts suspended simultaneously until the parent organization resolves the issue.
