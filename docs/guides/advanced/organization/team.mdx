# Enterprise Team Management

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization/team`

Invite corporate staff, regional directors, and finance managers to the Organization Portal.

## Roles and Cross-Entity Access

Users invited to the Organization portal inherently have a wider scope of access than those invited to a specific Merchant portal.

- **Organization Admin:** Can manage all enterprise settings, documents, and view aggregated data from all attached merchants.
- **Financial Controller:** Can view consolidated sales reports, payouts, and master invoices, but cannot alter operational settings or create new merchants.
- **Regional Manager:** Can be assigned a subset of merchants to monitor, providing a localized management layer.

> [!TIP]
> Do not invite store-level employees (like cashiers or warehouse staff) to the Organization Portal. Invite them directly to the specific Merchant Portal they work for via `(merchant) > /merchant/team`.
