# Enterprise Settings

**Route:** `(organization) > /organization/settings`

Configure high-level operational rules that cascade down to attached entities.

## Configuration Areas

- **Sub-Merchant Creation:** If enabled by the Workspace Admin, you can configure whether your regional managers can independently create new sub-merchants or if they require approval from the Organization Admin.
- **Data Silos (Multi-tenant isolation):** Toggle whether staff assigned to the Organization level can see aggregated data from *all* merchants, or if they must be explicitly assigned to specific merchants.
- **API Access:** Generate Enterprise API keys to connect the platform to your corporate ERP (like SAP or Oracle) to pull consolidated sales data.
