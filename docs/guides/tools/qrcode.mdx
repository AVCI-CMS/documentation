# QRCode Generator

**Menu Path:** `Tools > QRCode`

The **QRCode** generator allows you to easily bridge offline marketing (flyers, billboards, physical store displays) with your online store.

## Static vs. Dynamic QR Codes

- **Static QR:** Contains the actual URL hardcoded into the visual pattern. If you print 10,000 flyers and the URL breaks, the flyers are ruined.
- **Dynamic QR (Recommended):** The pattern contains a short link generated by AVCI CMS (e.g., `qr.yourstore.com/123`). The CMS then redirects the user to the final destination. 

## Creating a Dynamic QR Code

1. Click **"Create"**.
2. **Destination URL:** Where do you want the user to end up? (e.g., a specific Campaign page or Product).
3. **Label:** Give it an internal name (e.g., "Summer Magazine Ad - Page 5").
4. **Generate & Download:** The system generates the square image. You can download it as an SVG or high-resolution PNG for your graphic designer to put in a magazine.

### Analytics and Updating
- Because it is dynamic, you can click on the QR code in the CMS and **change the destination URL** at any time. The printed magazine ad will instantly start redirecting people to the new link.
- You can also view exact scan statistics (How many people scanned it? On what days?) to measure the ROI of your offline marketing campaigns.
